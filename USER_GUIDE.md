## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up**
   - Visit your Apply AI instance
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
   - Enter your email address
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Complete your profile

2. **Verify Your Email**
   - Click the verification link in your email
   - You'll be redirected to the login page
   - Sign in with your credentials

3. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your name and photo
   - Set your job title
   - Configure notification preferences
   - Set your timezone

### First Login

When you first log in, you'll see the Dashboard with:
- Quick actions to create your first job
- Tour of main features
- Links to this user guide

## 💼 Managing Jobs

### Creating a New Job

1. **Navigate to Jobs**
   - Click "Jobs" in the main navigation
   - Click "Create New Job" button

2. **Fill in Job Details**
   ```
   Required Fields:
   - Job Title
   - Description
   - Employment Type
   - Location (or Remote)
   
   Optional Fields:
   - Salary Range
   - Requirements
   - Benefits
   - Company Culture
   - Application Deadline
   ```

3. **Set Job Requirements**
   - Add required skills (e.g., "React", "TypeScript")
   - Specify experience level
   - Add educational requirements
   - Include certifications if needed

4. **Configure Application Form**
   - Choose which fields to require
   - Add custom questions
   - Enable resume upload
   - Set cover letter requirement

5. **Publish or Save as Draft**
   - Click "Save as Draft" to review later
   - Click "Publish" to make live immediately

### Editing a Job

1. Find the job in the Jobs list
2. Click the three-dot menu → "Edit"
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Closing a Job

1. Find the job in the Jobs list
2. Click the three-dot menu → "Close Job"
3. Confirm the action
4. Applications will be preserved but new ones won't be accepted

### Sharing Job Postings

Each job has a unique shareable URL:
1. Open the job details
2. Click "Share" button
3. Copy the URL
4. Share on social media, job boards, or via email

## 📋 Reviewing Applications

### Application List View

The Applications page shows all received applications with:
- Candidate name and photo
- Applied position
- Application date
- Current status
- AI score (if available)

### Filtering Applications

Use filters to find specific applications:
- **By Status**: Pending, Reviewing, Interviewed, Offered, etc.
- **By Job**: Filter to specific position
- **By Date**: Applications from specific date range
- **By Score**: Filter by AI score range

### Viewing Application Details

Click on any application to see:
- Candidate contact information
- Resume and cover letter
- Custom question responses
- AI analysis and scoring
- Application timeline
- Notes from team members

### Changing Application Status

1. Open the application
2. Click the status dropdown
3. Select new status:
   - **Pending**: Newly received, not yet reviewed
   - **Reviewing**: Currently under review
   - **Interviewed**: Interview completed
   - **Offered**: Job offer extended
   - **Hired**: Candidate accepted offer
   - **Rejected**: Not moving forward

4. Optionally add a note explaining the decision

### Adding Notes

Keep track of your thoughts and team feedback:
1. Open the application
2. Scroll to Notes section
3. Click "Add Note"
4. Type your note
5. Click "Save"

Notes are visible to all team members with access.

## 🤖 AI-Powered Features

### Automatic Candidate Scoring

Apply AI uses AI to automatically score candidates:

**What is Scored:**
- Technical skills match
- Experience relevance
- Education alignment
- Communication quality (from cover letter)
- Problem-solving indicators
- Cultural fit potential

**Score Breakdown:**
- **80-100**: Excellent match
- **60-79**: Good match
- **40-59**: Moderate match
- **0-39**: Poor match

### Viewing AI Analysis

1. Open any application
2. Look for the "AI Analysis" card
3. Review:
   - Overall score
   - Score breakdown by category
   - Identified strengths
   - Areas of concern
   - Recommendations

### Requesting Re-Analysis

If you've updated job requirements or want a fresh analysis:
1. Open the application
2. Click "Reanalyze" button in AI Analysis card
3. Wait for new results (usually 10-30 seconds)

### AI-Generated Insights

The AI provides:
- **Strengths**: Candidate's strong points
- **Concerns**: Potential weaknesses or gaps
- **Recommendations**: Suggested next steps

### Bias Detection

Apply AI monitors for potential bias in:
- Screening patterns
- Rejection reasons
- Interview selections
- Hiring decisions

View bias analysis in Analytics → Bias Analysis

## 📅 Interview Scheduling

### Scheduling an Interview

1. **From Application View**
   - Open the application
   - Click "Schedule Interview" button
   - Select interview type (Phone, Video, In-Person)
   - Choose date and time
   - Add interviewers
   - Add location or meeting link
   - Add notes or agenda
   - Click "Schedule"

2. **Interview Confirmation**
   - Candidate receives email notification
   - Calendar invitation sent
   - Interviewers notified

### Interview Types

- **Phone Screen**: Initial phone conversation
- **Video Interview**: Remote interview via video call
- **On-Site Interview**: In-person at office
- **Technical Assessment**: Coding or skills test
- **Panel Interview**: Multiple interviewers

### Managing Interviews

View all scheduled interviews in the Interviews section:
- Upcoming interviews
- Past interviews
- Cancelled interviews

### Rescheduling

1. Find the interview
2. Click "Reschedule"
3. Choose new date/time
4. All participants notified automatically

### Adding Interview Feedback

After an interview:
1. Open the application
2. Go to Interviews tab
3. Click "Add Feedback" for the interview
4. Fill in the feedback form:
   - Rating (1-5 stars)
   - Written feedback
   - Recommendation (Hire, Maybe, No)
5. Submit feedback

## 📈 Analytics & Reports

### Performance Metrics

Track your recruitment performance:
- Time to hire
- Application to hire ratio
- Interview to offer ratio
- Offer acceptance rate
- Source effectiveness

### Viewing Analytics

1. Navigate to Analytics
2. Choose a report type:
   - **Performance**: Overall recruitment metrics
   - **Predictions**: Hiring success forecasts
   - **Comparison**: Benchmark against similar roles
   - **Bias Analysis**: Fairness insights
   - **Model Insights**: AI model performance

3. Select date range
4. Apply filters (job, department, etc.)

### Exporting Reports

1. Open any analytics view
2. Click "Export" button
3. Choose format:
   - **PDF**: For presentations
   - **CSV**: For spreadsheet analysis
   - **JSON**: For custom processing
4. Download the file

### Custom Reports

Create custom reports:
1. Go to Analytics → Custom Reports
2. Click "Create Report"
3. Select metrics to include
4. Configure filters
5. Save report for future use

## 👥 Team Collaboration

### Inviting Team Members

1. Go to Settings → Team
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email address
4. Select role:
   - **Admin**: Full access
   - **Hiring Manager**: Manage jobs and applications
   - **Recruiter**: Review and manage applications
   - **Interviewer**: View applications and add feedback
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access
5. Click "Send Invitation"

### Managing Permissions

Control what each role can do:

| Permission | Admin | Manager | Recruiter | Interviewer | Viewer |
|------------|-------|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Create jobs | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Edit jobs | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| View applications | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Change status | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Schedule interviews | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| View analytics | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Manage team | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

### Assigning Reviewers

Assign specific team members to review applications:
1. Open an application
2. Click "Assign Reviewer"
3. Select team member(s)
4. They'll receive a notification

### Commenting and @mentions

Collaborate on applications:
1. Add a comment in any application
2. Use @mention to tag team members
3. Tagged members receive notifications

## 📱 Mobile Usage

### Installing as App

Apply AI can be installed as a native app:

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Apply AI in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android:**
1. Open Apply AI in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Desktop:**
1. Look for the install icon in the address bar
2. Click "Install"
3. App opens in its own window

### Offline Mode

When installed, Apply AI works offline:
- View cached applications
- Add notes (syncs when online)
- View analytics
- Schedule interviews (syncs when online)

Your changes will sync automatically when you're back online.

### Mobile Features

All desktop features are available on mobile:
- Review applications
- Change statuses
- Add notes
- Schedule interviews
- View analytics

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### For Faster Screening

1. **Use AI Scoring**
   - Let AI do initial screening
   - Focus on high-scoring candidates first
   - Review AI recommendations

2. **Set Up Filters**
   - Create saved filters for common searches
   - Use status filters to organize workflow
   - Filter by score range

3. **Use Keyboard Shortcuts**
   - `n`: Next application
   - `p`: Previous application
   - `s`: Change status
   - `i`: Schedule interview

### For Better Candidate Experience

1. **Respond Quickly**
   - Aim to review within 48 hours
   - Keep candidates updated on status
   - Be transparent about timeline

2. **Personalize Communication**
   - Reference specific details from their application
   - Explain rejection reasons kindly
   - Provide feedback when possible

3. **Clear Job Descriptions**
   - Be specific about requirements
   - Include salary range if possible
   - Highlight company culture

### For Team Collaboration

1. **Use @mentions**
   - Tag specific people for input
   - Ask questions in comments
   - Keep discussions in context

2. **Add Detailed Notes**
   - Document reasoning for decisions
   - Share interview feedback promptly
   - Track concerns and strengths

3. **Assign Reviewers**
   - Distribute workload evenly
   - Assign based on expertise
   - Set review deadlines

### For Data-Driven Hiring

1. **Check Analytics Regularly**
   - Monitor time to hire
   - Track source effectiveness
   - Review bias metrics

2. **Trust the AI, but Verify**
   - Use AI scores as a guide
   - Always review personally
   - Provide feedback on AI accuracy

3. **Iterate and Improve**
   - Review what worked
   - Update job descriptions
   - Refine screening criteria
